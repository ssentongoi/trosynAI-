The Problem

Cloud AI creates friction for sensitive business work.

Teams want AI for contracts, reports, invoices, policies, and internal knowledge. The problem is that sensitive workflows often require stronger control over where data is processed, who governs it, and whether work can continue when connectivity is unreliable.

🔒
Sensitive data needs direct control
Legal, finance, HR, and security teams need AI assistance without making external cloud systems the default place for internal documents and workflow context.
🏛️
Governance becomes harder in the cloud
When data is processed through cloud AI systems, retention, deletion, and governance are influenced by provider policies and legal obligations outside the organization’s direct control.
📡
Cloud dependency limits operations
In Africa, parts of Asia and South America, and remote operating sites, unreliable connectivity can make cloud-first AI unavailable exactly when teams need it.

AI that runs inside your organization’s environment.

Trosyn AI packages private AI, document workflows, access controls, and deployment support into one system. Teams get a simple interface while the organization keeps control over where processing happens.

Deployment Modes

One system, deployed at the right scale.

Trosyn AI starts simple and scales with the organization. Device and Team deployments are the near-term focus. Enterprise deployment is roadmap only.

Device Deployment · SME
Private AI on laptops and desktops
For individuals and small teams that need private document processing without external infrastructure. Designed for self-serve installation and offline-capable workflows.
Entry point for small teams and professional service firms.
Team Deployment · Mid-market
Shared AI for internal teams
For growing teams that need shared access, reusable workflow context, and broader document collaboration inside a controlled environment.
Primary commercial focus.
Enterprise Deployment · Roadmap Only
Internal server deployment
For larger organizations that need organization-wide private AI inside internal server environments. This is a later-stage deployment path.
Introduced after smaller deployments prove reliability.
